HOLIDAY

Explore Jombang, Oct 16, 2020

This photo was taken during my trip to explore Jombang. I was accompanied by two of my female cousins and my uncle around the city of Jombang. There we went to several places such as Islamic boarding schools and modern cafes which were very aesthetic. There are still many gardens there unlike in Surabaya. In the evening I returned to the house that could be called the "village" of Jombang. The house I live in is surrounded by gardens. The morning air there is very fresh and strangely even though it is surrounded by gardens but there are no mosquitoes so I can sleep soundly.
